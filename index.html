ABOUT GOJO

Gojo Satoru is a prominent character in the manga and anime series "Jujutsu Kaisen," created by Gege Akutami. Revered as one of the most powerful and enigmatic Jujutsu sorcerers in the series, Gojo serves as a teacher and mentor at the Tokyo Metropolitan Jujutsu Technical High School. Known for his charismatic and carefree demeanor, Gojo conceals an unparalleled skill in combat and possesses a deep understanding of Jujutsu techniques.
With his iconic blindfold covering his eyes, Gojo is recognized for his extraordinary abilities, including the Limitless Cursed Technique and the Six Eyes, making him a formidable force against Cursed Spirits. His dedication to protecting students and combating the Cursed Spirits that threaten humanity highlights his role as a central figure in the fight against dark forces.
