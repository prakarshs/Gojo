GOJO SATORU

五条悟

Gojo Satoru is a powerful and mysterious jujutsu sorcerer in the manga and anime series "Jujutsu Kaisen." As the strongest teacher at Tokyo Metropolitan Jujutsu Technical High School, he mentors the main characters and is known for his charismatic and carefree demeanor. Despite appearing laid-back with his blindfold, Gojo possesses immense strength, using the Limitless Cursed Technique and Six Eyes. His unconventional teaching style and dedication to protecting his students make him a central and beloved character in the series.
